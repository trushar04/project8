Unlocking Paradise: Eternal Beauty Carved in Marble

The architects mastered proportions and tricks of the eye. As you approach, the Taj appears close and large, but it shrinks as you get nearer. The minarets lean outward for both aesthetics and safety. The gruesome tale of Shah Jahan blinding artisans to prevent replication lacks evidence. Yet, it adds drama to the romantic tragedy.
